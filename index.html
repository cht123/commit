A philosophy of becoming

LYVO is built on behavioral psychology, not gamification. Here's what makes it different.

Identity over goals

Traditional apps track what you do. LYVO focuses on who you're becoming. When you commit to being "someone who exercises," the behavior follows naturally.

Consistency compounds

Showing up matters more than performance. A 15-second reflection on a hard day is worth more than a perfect day you never acknowledge.

Reflection deepens change

Quick daily reflections create awareness. Awareness creates intention. Intention becomes identity. The cycle reinforces itself.

Intentionally minimal

  • One commitment at a time — focus creates clarity
  • 15-second daily reflections — brief but meaningful
  • Gentle reminders — morning, midday, or evening
  • Rolling archive — see your journey unfold
  • Privacy-first — your reflections stay on your device

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

LYVO ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Don't Collect

LYVO is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect usage analytics or behavioral data
  • We do not use third-party tracking or advertising services
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Data Stored on Your Device

All your commitments, reflections, and app preferences are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. This includes:

  • Your commitment text and history
  • Daily reflection entries
  • Notification preferences and times
  • App settings and preferences

In-App Purchases

LYVO offers optional premium features through Apple's App Store. All purchase transactions are processed by Apple and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your payment information.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security features, including encryption and your device passcode or biometric authentication.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the LYVO app from your device. Since no data is stored on external servers, deletion is complete and permanent.

Children's Privacy

LYVO is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated revision date.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my commitment?

You can archive your current commitment and start a new one at any time. Tap on your commitment to access options, or start fresh from the home screen.

What happens to my data if I delete the app?

All your data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, your data is permanently removed. We don't store anything on external servers.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Go to Settings within the app and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

Can I use LYVO on multiple devices?

Your premium purchase works across all your iOS devices signed into the same Apple ID. However, since data is stored locally, your commitments and history won't sync between devices.
